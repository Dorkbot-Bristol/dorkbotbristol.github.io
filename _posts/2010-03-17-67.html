    "Vocal Trio," which consists of three musical instruments, transforms the voice of a participant received through a mike into a sound of specific musical instrument while maintaining the same rhythm and musical scale. It also transforms the physical activity of speaking as a form of communication in everyday life into a non-linguistic form of communication by means of the sound of musical instruments.
