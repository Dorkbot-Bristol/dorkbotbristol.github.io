It's Christmas, so time for an extra special Dorkbot - we'll have talks, music, and a bar. Free entry, no registration required, bring your own refreshments. Talks start 7.30pm, doors from 6.45pm. We'll probably run later than a regular Dorkbot. Please feel free to bring interesting things along to show others - things you've built, things to play, or curiosities to discuss. Don't forget to join our mailing list, and follow us on Twitter.
