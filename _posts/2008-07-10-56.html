--- layout: post number: 56 start: 2008-07-10 19:00 end: 2008-07-10 23:00 where: limehouse_town_hall tags: - Joel Gethin Lewis - Sarah Angliss - Harold Cohen --- Featuring:
  • Contact - Joel Gethin Lewis
    Joel will be talking about Contact, an UVA installation, and how OpenFrameworks enabled them to make the project so fast.
  • Wireless handbells - Sarah Angliss
    Sarah will be showing her carillon, a mediaeval instrument used to play sacred music, augmented using xbee technology. It consists of 30 handbells, scattered around the room, and controlled using a theremin and a mini wireless network (also showing at the Sonic Arts Expo, Brighton, Saturday 5 July).
  • AARON - Harold Cohen
    The AARON software has been continually developed since 1973, hand crafted by Harold Cohen to generate his paintings. Harold joins us while visiting the UK for his exhibition at the Bernard Jacobson Gallery (running from 9th July - 15th August).
