With short talks (each <20 min) and demos from...
==== Tim Hunkin ==== http://www.timhunkin.com/ "Celebrities, Zombies and Bedbugs" - Tim is an English engineer,
cartoonist, writer, and artist. He created the Channel Four TV series
and Science Museum gallery The Secret Life of Machines, explaining the
workings and history of various household devices.

==== Saskia Freeke ==== http://www.sasj.nl/ Saskia is a lecturer in Physical Computing at Goldsmiths, an artist,
creative coder and visual/interaction designer. She creates interactive
playful installations that explore how we interact with technology.

==== James Larsson ==== http://jameslarsson.com/ The inventor, techno-humorist and Dorkbot London regular returns with
more on the past and future of his £200 Flashing Light Prize
- plus "a moderately silly/destructive hack of old office equipment."

==== Kaylene Kau ==== https://www.kaylenekau.com/ Animal Diplomacy Bureau's mission is to cultivate better human-animal
relations through changing the way we think. The first project by the
Bureau transforms people into London Birds in a series of mixed
reality games - to understand how urban environments are also home to
a wide variety of wildlife.

==== Open dorks/demos: ==== Bob Sturm
https://highnoongmt.wordpress.com/ New Xmas carols composed with the help of a deep neural network.

Holistic Howling
http://holistichowling.com Ian Morrison reintroduces Holistic Howling, the low tech alternative to
high tech trouble, and how it's helping geeks around the world handle
