With short talks and demos from... ==== Sian Fan ==== "Seeping Out: Virtuality Beyond the Screen" Sian Fan will be analysing and exploring how new technologies act as portals into digital realms. She will explore how the screen sits as a barrier between two worlds, mediating, interrupting, and ultimately dividing the physical world from the digital dimension. She will discuss the commonalities between digital spaces and dream spaces, analysing how both act as distortions of our waking reality, enabling escapism and for the impossible to be realised, but not necessarily satisfying. Sian Fan is an emerging artist working in digital media, installation and performance. She is currently studying a Fine Art Masters at Central Saint Martins. Sian works with movement, the female body and technology to explore and expose what it means to exist, both physically and spiritually. She explores the relationship between the natural and the digital through 3D scanning, animation and virtual reality. sianfan.com / @sianfan ==== Shardcore ==== "Fake it ’til you make it - creating digital lies" shardcore.org / @erocdrahs ==== L H Trevail ==== “A Campfire in the Uncanny Valley” Horror and hope in tech, and how artists are making ghosts of ourselves by mistake. lhtrevail.co.uk / @lhtrevail ==== Dr. Elif Ozden Yenigun ==== This project has emerged in the career of two researchers as a consequence of over-priced printers, and high material specs in the aerospace industry associated with expensive research costs, that lead to innovative artefacts and affordable self-built 3D printers.
