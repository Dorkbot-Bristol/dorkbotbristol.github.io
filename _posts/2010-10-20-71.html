  • The Paradox of Public Domain Invention - Cefn Hoile
    Cefn Hoile presents the Enigmaker project, a model of public-domain invention inspired by open source hardware and software. Over two months in a mobile-home-lab, a new invention a week was documented. Only one detail of each prototype was left out - what's it for? Visitors and twitter users were invited to make their guesses. Enigmaker's #project3 was a laser-cut, perpetual 4-week-horizon wall planner. The 'Tacticalendar' is now ready for production. The first batch has been mailed to customers, with the design simultaneously published under a creative commons license. http://tacticalendar.co.uk/
